Volunteer Work
IRIS (ICU Refugee Immigrant Solidarity) is a student-led organization that an underclassman and I founded at ICU (International Christian University) in 2021. The organization aims to provide support to Refugees and Immigrants who are living in Tokyo.

Activities of the organization include Food Drives, Refugee Cafes, Translational Services, Seminars, Detention Center visits, and spreading awareness regarding the issues surrounding Refugees and Immigrants across Japan.
The organization has been featured in local newspapers and university newsletters and currently boasts 40+ members.
